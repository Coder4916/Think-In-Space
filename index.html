Over the last 7 years Elwyn Davies has been employed as a learning support co-ordinator working within the MOD. His clientele has included people from varied backgrounds and ethnicities. Elwyn has been able to foster a trusting and safe environment for his clients, giving them the opportunity to share their thoughts/worries/concerns. Elwyn's work has been particularly effective when working alongside those with learning differences i.e. Neurodiverse, whilst utilising coaching techniques underpinned by visual thinking strategies Mind Mapping and a no excuses policy.

"I learnt that if you trust and challenge people to rewrite their story they are often very capable of becoming independant learners who can become their own solution".

Elwyn can draw from a vast pool of life skills and experience, having served in the Royal Air Force for 22 years, he has leadership experience in both the Military and Civil Service.
