Why Life Coaching?

Have you ever thought that you're not fulfilling your potential? Perhaps you would like to be better at something such as a skill or hobby? Do you want to make better use of your attributes or perform better during stressful situations? Have you ever looked at another person and wondered how they are able to remain so composed or knowledgable? What if that was you but you just couldn't see it!"

The truth is we often over over-estimate the capabilities of others while de-valuing our own strengths. Whether it is a work or social interaction or dealing with the challenges of life, you have the resources to be a better 'you'.

You already possess the necessary skills to overcome the obstacles that you may have thought were too difficult. You have the in-built ability to be successful. Think-In-Space could help you realise what you are capable of and to harness your abilities to release your potential.

"I hope that you choose me to help you navigate that journey."

What to expect...

Your journey with Think-In-Space begins with an informal, flexible introductory session, led by you in a neutral setting of your choice which includes:

  • A chance to build rapport and find out if this type of coaching is for you.
  • Discussing any boundaries and code of ethics.
  • Explaining what you would like to get out of any available coaching sessions.
  • Initialising a record/review of any progress made.
  • Agree on any self help exercises, if relevant, through discussion/Q and A.
  • Afterthoughts and followup if required.
"My approach is flexible based on my client's needs."
