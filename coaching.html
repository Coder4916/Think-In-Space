Is coaching for me?

Have you ever thought that you're not fulfilling your potential? That you would like to be better at something, perhaps improve on a skill or hobby? Do you want to make better use of your strengths or be calmer under pressure? Have you ever looked at another person and wondered how they are able to be so composed or knowledgable? What if that was you but you just couldn't see it!

The truth is we often over estimate the capabilities of others while de-valuing our own strengths. For example, how many times have you criticised yourself with harsh phrases and then wonder why you don't succeed. The truth is that we all need to get out of own way occasionally so that we can re-write our life story and start to be our own best friend rather than our worst critic.

Whether it is a work or social interaction or dealing with the challenges of life, you have the resources to be a better 'you'. You already possess the necessary skills to overcome the obstacles that you may have thought were too difficult. You have the in-built ability to be successful. Think In Space could help you realise what you are capable of and to harness your abilities to release your potential.

I hope that you choose me to help you navigate that journey.

What to expect...

Your journey with Think In Space begins with an informal, flexible introductory session, led by you in a setting of your choice which includes:

  • A chance to build rapport and find out if this type of coaching is for you.
  • Discussing any boundaries and code of ethics.
  • Explaining what you would like to get out of any available coaching sessions.
  • Begin to put any plans in place
  • Initialising a record/review of any progress made.
  • Agree on any self help exercises, if relevant, through discussion/Q and A.
  • Sharing insights from the session and define any further next steps.
  • Acknowledgment of your journey and self reflection.
